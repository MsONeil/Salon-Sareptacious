Mission

Our mission is to create personalised client looks from the most on trend cutting, colouring and finishing techniques. We only use high quailty products imported from Italy. Our Stylists are regularly attending up-to date educational classes and are exctied for expanding knowledge of their craft.We are located in the small town of New Sarepta Alberta but that doesn't mean you don't get a full-service salon experience . We carry only the best professional products... Davines.

We are a proud member of Green Circle Salons, and proud to use and provide Davines sustainable products to reduce our carbon footprint. Davines was founded in Parma, Italy in 1983 by the Bollati family. Davines Group started as a research laboratory, producing high-end hair care products for renowned cosmetic companies worldwide.
